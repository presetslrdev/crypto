Buy and sell cryptocurrency easy

CryptoCourse is a crypto wallet created as a result of cooperation between BSB Bank and Currency.com.

With CryptoCourse the purchase and sale of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies becomes as simple and accessible as transactions with US Dollar or Euro.

Store cryptocurrency in your own wallet

CryptoCourse wallet is the optimal solution for regular transactions with cryptocurrencies and tokens and their long-term storage.

Your funds are under reliable bank protection

Your fiat balance in the CryptoCourse App is a real bank account under your name.

CryptoCourse doesn’t keep your money and entrusts this important function to the bank.
